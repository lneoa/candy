Untitled

Inhale all the air in the room
Delete existence
Black hole, flood the page
So that the ink never dries
Stay mad forever
Protest earthquakes

Suspension is sweet
So haunt your loved ones
And ascend like crude oil
Rain amuse bouche

Powerful men want and have everything
I just want to have one thing
All the air in the room
